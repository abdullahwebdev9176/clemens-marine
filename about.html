About Clemens Marina

Since 1958, many things have changed...Clemens Marina's commitment to offering the industry leading aluminum boating brands and outboard manufacturers has not. Fast forward over 60 years and we have grown to be the largest aluminum boat dealer in the Northwest, with three locations to better serve you.

Through the years we have had the privilege of partnering with all the finest marine brands. From our recent addition of the Godfrey Pontoon boat line, to the ever-increasing demand for larger offshore series boats offered by North River, Hewescraft and Stabicraft (and every size in-between), we got you covered!. Our award-winning service by factory trained technicians and a very experienced support team is a long-lasting relationship, not just a boat sale.

A Commitment to Our Boating Community

Clemens Marina is built on lasting relationships with our customers and the boating community we serve. We believe in honest guidance, personalized support, and attention to detail in everything we do. By combining industry expertise with a genuine dedication to customer satisfaction, we strive to deliver an exceptional experience from your first visit to every adventure that follows.

WELCOME TO Clemens Marina

Founded in 1958, Clemens Marina has grown into one of the Pacific Northwest’s most trusted marine dealerships and the region’s largest aluminum boat dealer. With multiple locations across Oregon, Clemens Marina specializes in rugged, performance-driven boats built for fishing, offshore adventures, and family recreation. Their reputation is built on industry-leading brands, expert guidance, and award-winning service from factory-trained technicians who support customers long after the sale. Committed to quality products, cutting-edge technology, and lasting customer relationships, Clemens Marina delivers a complete boating experience designed for confidence on the water.

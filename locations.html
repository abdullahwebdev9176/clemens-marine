Eugene Location

What Stands Out

Our Eugene location is Clemens Marina's flagship facility, serving boating enthusiasts throughout the Willamette Valley since 1958. Strategically positioned near the Willamette River, this location combines decades of maritime expertise with modern amenities to provide an unmatched boat buying and ownership experience.

  • State-of-the-art service facility with 12,000 sq ft of workspace
  • Largest inventory of aluminum boats in the Willamette Valley
  • On-site parts department with extensive stock
  • Climate-controlled indoor showroom featuring 20+ boats
  • Factory-certified technicians with 100+ years combined experience
  • Convenient access to the Willamette River and nearby lakes
  • Full rigging and custom installation services available
